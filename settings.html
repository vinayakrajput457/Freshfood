Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Click on the Register button in the navigation bar and fill in your details. Verify your email and you're ready to go!

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, digital wallets, and cash on delivery.

Can I schedule a delivery?

Yes! During checkout, you can select "Schedule for Later" to choose a specific delivery time.

How do I use promo codes?

In your cart, find the "Promo Code" section, enter your code, and click Apply. The discount will be applied instantly.

What if my order arrives late?

Contact our support team immediately. We'll investigate and provide a refund or replacement.

Do you offer dietary options?

Yes! Check product filters for vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and other dietary options.
